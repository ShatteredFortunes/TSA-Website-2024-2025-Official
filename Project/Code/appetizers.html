    BRUSCHETTA

    Toasted baguette with light and refreshing tomato, garlic, and balsamic glaze.
    Fun Fact: The roots date back to Ancient Rome and possibly Ancient Greece. It was believed that olive growers would toast bread to test their freshly pressed olive oils to test the quality. Its name comes from the Romanesco dialect verb "bruscare", which means "to roast over coals", which reflects the traditional way of preparing the dish.


    $5.99, 12 pc



    Contains: gluten
